About Me

Hi, I'm Manuel Hernandez. Originally from Bryan/College Station and raised in Houston, I'm pursuing a career as a professional graphic designer. Art has always been my way of expressing what words sometimes can't. I enjoy blending creativity with purposeful design to create visually engaging and meaningful work that brings ideas to life. I'm constantly exploring new techniques and trends to grow my skills and keep my work fresh and inspiring.
